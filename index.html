Strava Integration

Upload your Apple Health workouts directly to Strava. Connect once, then upload any workout with a single tap. No files to manage, no middleman.

1. Connect

Tap "Connect with Strava" using the official OAuth flow

2. Log in

Sign in to your Strava account securely on strava.com

3. Authorize

Review permissions and authorize SnapGPX to upload activities

4. Ready

Your account is connected. Upload workouts anytime with one tap

How it works

  • OAuth 2.0 authentication with activity:write scope
  • Uploads GPX files to Strava as new activities
  • Automatic activity type mapping (running, cycling, hiking, swimming, and more)
  • Multisport/triathlon workouts are split into separate Strava activities by discipline
  • One-way upload only — SnapGPX does not read or download data from Strava

Your data stays safe

  • Your Strava credentials are never seen or stored by SnapGPX
  • OAuth tokens stored securely in your device's Keychain
  • No backend server — all processing happens on your iPhone
  • Only GPX data (GPS route + timestamps + elevation) is sent to Strava
  • Disconnect anytime — all tokens are immediately deleted from your device
